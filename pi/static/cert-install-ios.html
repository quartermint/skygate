Install Certificate (iOS)

3 quick steps to enable Max Savings on your iPhone or iPad

1

Download Certificate Profile

Tap the button below, then tap Allow when prompted to download the profile.

Download Profile
2

Install Profile

Open Settings > General > VPN & Device Management

Tap SkyGate Max Savings, then tap Install.

Enter your passcode if prompted.

3

Enable Full Trust

Open Settings > General > About > Certificate Trust Settings

Enable full trust for SkyGate CA Certificate.

This step is required. iOS does not trust custom CA certificates by default, even after installing the profile.
Done - Go to Dashboard Back to mode selection

Remove SkyGate Certificate After Your Flight

The SkyGate CA certificate is only needed during your flight. Remove it when you land.

 iOS / iPadOS

Open Settings > General > VPN & Device Management

Tap SkyGate Max Savings, then tap Remove Profile.

🤖 Android

Open Settings > Security > Encryption & Credentials > Trusted Credentials

Go to the User tab, find SkyGate, and tap Remove.

💻 macOS

Open Keychain Access (Applications > Utilities).

In the System keychain, find SkyGate CA and delete it.

🖥 Windows

Press Win+R, type certmgr.msc, press Enter.

Navigate to Trusted Root Certification Authorities > Certificates.

Find SkyGate CA, right-click, and select Delete.

If you used Quick Connect mode, no certificate was installed and no action is needed.
